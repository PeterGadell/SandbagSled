Frequently asked Questions

01 What is included with the purchase of The Sandbag Sled?

When you purchase The Sandbag Sled you will receive the sled attachment along with two securing rods to affix the sled attachment to your sandbag.

02 Is the sandbag included?

No, The Sandbag Sled is designed to securely attach to your existing sandbag. The Sandbag Sled works with most sandbag brands.

03 Are straps or other accessories included?

No, The Sandbag Sled protects your sandbag as it is dragged. It is designed to be used in tandem with a variety of accessories.

04 Does The Sandbag Sled work with any sandbag?

The Sandbag Sled attaches to most sandbag brands. The sled affixes to the sandbag using the end handles on the sandbag. The sandbag must have these handles to be compatible. See digital content for more information on how The Sandbag Sled attaches to a sandbag.

05 What surfaces can I use The Sandbag Sled on?

The Sandbag Sled can be used on a variety of non-abrasive and minimally abrasive surfaces. Tested surfaces include grass, turf, dirt, sand, and smooth concrete. Individual results may vary dependent on terrain and composition of surface.

06 Is there a weight limit for The Sandbag Sled?

Since you are towing the weight with the sandbag handle rather than the sled, the weight limit for the product is limited only by the weight capacity of the sandbag.
